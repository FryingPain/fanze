My experience with my previous school

Over the past few years, I've been on the same school, until 2023, where i switched school. It was a very nice school, where i met friends, some still in contact to this day, but i had to abandon that school.
Why is that you may ask? Well, it begins at the start of the COVID-19 pandemic.

Everything stated here is from my own and personal experience, i wont include incidents that happened to others because believe me, it also happened and way worse.

2020

The pandemic

At early 2020, classes were cut due to the virus spreading in my country. As a result, our school implemented the famous online classes system.
This was the first cause of chaos.

2021

The pandemic: Phase 2

Classes were still cancelled, and this year was online classes as well.
Over the course of these 2 years of online classes, my classmates behavior started to worsen, due to them forgetting the values and going through puberty.

I've been also showing an interest in furries, which would develop into me being a furry, my fursona was a green Protogen. This state was the recipe of disaster.

By the end of the year, a few classmates starting going to physical school.

2022

The chaos

My classmates, after 2 years without interaction to people, and their hormones reaching peak limits, started showing their real side when they arrived school.

A certain classmate noticed i am a furry, and started to disapprove me, hating speech, my best friend also did the same. It really hurted me, because of my preferences. No need to say i was also being bullied online for this.

Then a classmate thought i was flirting with his girlfriend. I was in no way flirting with her, we both wee just good friends, all i did was just normal friends stuff like hugging.

I had a talk with the "principal" about the previous student bullying me for being a furry. To me, he sounded like "it's better to not be a furry cause they're weird". After that, i completely quit my fursona and being a furry. Now seeing them makes me uneasy and mad.

My teacher also threatened me to leave a negative note for banging the table in a moment of brief frustration. I have Asperger's syndrome, although i do believe my actions in that moment were disrespectful, i also believe i should have been treated nicely instead of threat and anger.
It felt like that my teacher unleashed all the anger she had with my classmates bad behavior on me, which in comparison to them, I'm very nice.
I am still scared of her to this day, and from what i was told, she became more tedious.

Then the kid who thought i was flirting with his gf started to say a few stuff on my back. It wasn't any bad stuff, but it was the last straw for me in my opinion.

2023

The aftermath

On September 2022 i quit my school. I didnt go to school for the rest of the year. I am now in another school, and you wont believe how happy i have become in comparison to last year.

My friend also switched to this school, and now another friend who had been targeted for severe bullying is considering to switch to my school as well.

Conclusion

Thanks previous school for making me meet new friends and have great moments, but you have unfortunately changed and not for the better. It is sad to see that a school i was in since 2nd grade make this bad move.
